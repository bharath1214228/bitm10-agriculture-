List of Wireless Companies in India | Top Wireless Companies in India

The Wireless companies in India are envisioned to see the major spike in the production of wireless technologies. At present, these Indian wireless companies are the pioneers of wireless technology.

Wireless technology is adapted by the various industry sectors such as telecom companies, IoT solution providers, broadband service providers, or other cellular telecommunication services.

In 2019, the market of Indian wireless companies had total revenue of US $24 billion, representing a CARC of -4.7% from 2015 to 2019.

In addition, in the first quarter of FY22, the total wireless data usage in India increased 16.54% quarterly to reach 32,397 PB. During the third quarter of FY21, the contribution of 3G and 4G data usage to the total volume of wireless data usage was 1.78% & 97.74%,respectively. Besides this, the share of 2G data usage kept at 0.48% in the same quarter. (Source - IBEF)

In September 2021, the total wireless or mobile phone subscriber base reached 1166.02 million while it was 1,148.58 million in September 2020. This increment remarks a potential growth in the wireless industry.

Moreover, in September 2021, the total number of internet users was 794.88 million, out of this 787.94 million users were wireless internet users and the other 24.29 million users were wired internet users.

In India, during the next five years, increment in mobile-phone penetration and reduction in data costs will add 500 million new internet users. By 2025, India will require around 22 million skilled workers in 5G - centric technologies such as IoT, AI, cloud computing & robotics.
