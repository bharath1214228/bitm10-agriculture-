List of Warehousing Companies in India | Top Warehousing Companies in India

The warehousing companies in India were valued at INR 1,501.2 billion in 2019 and are expected to reach INR 2,821.1 billion by 2024, expanding at a CAGR of ~13.57% during 2020-2024 period.  Earlier logistics sector neglected among the ranked ones, now warehouses developed into a well designed and organized stockrooms with advanced state-of-the-art facilities like real-time tracking mechanisms in India.

Various initiatives taken by the Indian government have been driving the country's warehousing market towards growth, but investors had started taking cognizance of the sector much before the implementation of these reforms like granting infrastructure status to the logistics industry including warehousing. The warehousing market in India accounts for approximately 25% of the total logistics cost. (SOURCE Businesswire)

The growth of the warehousing sector in India is widely being impelled by the boost in the international market. Entry of domestic as well as foreign industries in India and formulation of manufacturing plants has created a great opportunity for export, that has turned the demand of warehouse to store the goods.

There are more than 572+ warehousing companies in India and over 11,000+ warehousing companies in the United States.

Nevertheless, scarcity of infrastructure has always been one of the biggest hurdles for the warehousing market in India. India has the smallest warehouse space with modern facilities in the whole world. Also, lack of networks, poor transport facilities, inability to handle high traffic density, and insufficient air and seaport capacities contribute to the increased cost per transaction in the country's logistics sector.

If you talk about the biggest warehouse in the world then it is Boeing Everett Factory, Washington on the other hand Container Corporation Of India Ltd is the largest in India.
